Morphic

Morphic is an upcoming video game coming in the 2030's, developed by Zapped Zaid. This game is a turn-based character-driven RPG with social simulation, dungeon crawling, creature collection, and Action RPG elements. The game also includes a very intense storyline that adapts to your choices as a player.

The World of Keroni

The game takes place in Keroni, a fictional nation with a new culture inspired by Japanese, Chinese, Indian, and Egyptian mythologies. Enjoy the streets' vast array of banana chips in Morphic.

But it is not deemed to be that simple. A murder series has taken over Keroni, and now you, along with your team of supernaturally powered humans who have been bestowed with the power of the morphic, try to figure out the mystery yourself.

About Transception Games

Transception Games was founded by Zapped Zaid with the vision of creating immersive and unique gaming experiences that blur the line between reality and fiction.

With Morphic being our flagship title, we aim to redefine the RPG genre by combining elements from various gameplay styles and creating a truly adaptive narrative experience.
